Saturday 2 Nov. 2013

Metallica admits to being 'Beliebers'

In an interview for Q Magazine, via Blabbermouth a series of subjects were discussed. Everything from the Black Album to Justin Bieber. When asked, if the thrashlegends Metallica were 'Beliebers', frontman James Hetfield shortly and simply replied 'yes.'

This is not something out of a april-fools article, with the intention of tricking the readers. Just to clear out any misconceptions right from the start. Hetfield did, believe it or not, answer that he was a Belieber, without any sarcasm. His answer was fully supported by his fellow bandmembers.

Saturday 2 Nov. 2013

Mötley Crüe adapts The Dirt to film

Mötley Crew have announced that their best-seller autobiography The Dirt-Confessions of the World's Most Notorious Rock Band will be adapted for the silver screen.

The book, which was released in 2001, was co-written by all four band-members and author Neil Strauss. The book spent 4 weeks on the New York Bestselles list, and sold 13,000 copies in the UK. Neil Strauss has also co-authored Marilyn Mansons autobiography The Long Hard Road Out of Hell. For the adaptation of the book, Mötley Crüe have hired TV and movie-director Jeff Tremaine..
