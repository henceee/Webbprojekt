Monday 4 Nov. 2013

Rob Zombie Preforms live at Jimmy Kimmel

Talkshow-host Jimmy Kimmel invited horror-rockers Rob Zombie to his Halloween Special of Jimmy Kimmel Live on ABC.

Rob Zombie recently their Summer Tour in North AmericaAmerica headlining the 2013 Rockstar Energy Drink Mayhem Festival. The band also filmed two sets in Houston and Dallas for an upcoming live DVD release.

On November 3rd they will preform along with Korn in Nevada, on their one-month long Night Of The Living Dreads Tour

On the show, Zombie preformed an cover of Grand Funk Railroad's "We're an American Band", as well as the new single "Dead City Radio And The New Gods Of Supertown".
